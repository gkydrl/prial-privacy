Frequently Asked Questions

What is Prial?

Prial is a price demand platform for Turkish e-commerce. You set the price you want to pay for a product, and we aggregate demand data from the community to help retailers understand real consumer price expectations.

How do I create a demand?

Search for a product, open the product detail page, drag the slider to your target price, and tap "Talep Oluştur". Your demand will be tracked and you'll receive a notification when the product reaches your target price.

How do I turn off notifications?

You can disable push notifications at any time through your iPhone Settings → Notifications → Prial, or from within the app under Settings.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at support@prial.io and we will process your request within 7 business days.

How do I update my demand?

Go to "Taleplerim" tab, find your demand, and tap on it to edit your target price or deactivate it.
