Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 09, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Prial ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information directly from you:

  • Email address
  • Username and password

We also collect the following information automatically:

  • Log and usage data (app activity, interactions, performance data)
  • Device data (device model, operating system, unique device identifiers)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Send push notifications about price changes and demand updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Identify usage trends and improve user experience
  • Send administrative and marketing communications

3. AI-Based Features

Prial uses AI-based technology to provide price drop predictions and demand insights. These features process aggregated, anonymized data to generate forecasts and analytics.

4. Push Notifications

We send push notifications to alert you when products reach your target price. You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings or within the app settings.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our infrastructure is hosted on Supabase, which provides industry-standard security including SSL encryption.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@prial.io.

8. Children's Privacy

Prial is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
